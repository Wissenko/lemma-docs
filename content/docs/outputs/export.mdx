title: Export evidence
description: Export Doc or Slides outputs when you need to share study findings outside Lemma.
---

Doc and Slides surfaces include an **Export** button.

Use export when the study output needs to be shared outside the Lemma workspace.

## Before Exporting

Check:

- the study title
- the sample details
- whether private participant information is visible
- quotes and examples
- recommendations
- whether the output is final enough to share

## Current note

The export button was observed during the product audit, but it was not clicked. Confirm export file formats and whether exporting consumes credits before documenting exact behavior.
