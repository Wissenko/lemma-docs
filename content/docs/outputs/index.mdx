Outputs are where Lemma becomes useful beyond the person who ran the study.

Use them after you have enough completed sessions to support a finding. Doc is best for a written research artifact. Slides are best when the finding needs to be presented. Exports are best when another tool or workflow needs the data.

## Before You Share

- check that the sample size and audience are clear
- verify important claims against transcripts or response summaries
- keep quotes tied to the participant context you are comfortable sharing
- avoid treating generated synthesis as final when the evidence is thin
