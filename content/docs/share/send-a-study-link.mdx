---
title: Send a study link
description: Share a Lemma study with participants and manage the collection flow.
---

Use a study link when you want participants to complete the study on their own time.

On the Studies dashboard, each editable study has a **Study actions** menu. In the audit, this menu included **Copy link**, **Duplicate**, **Pause**, **Archive**, and **Delete**.

## When to send a link

Send a link when:

- you have tested or previewed the study
- the goal is clear
- the interaction format is right for the audience
- the follow-up depth matches the task
- you know how you will use the responses

## What to include in the invite

Tell participants:

- why you are asking
- whether they will answer through text or voice
- approximately how long it will take
- that Lemma may ask follow-up questions
- how their answers will be used

Example:

> We are using Lemma to collect short interview-style feedback. It should take about five minutes. You will answer a few questions, and Lemma may ask follow-ups so we can understand the details behind your answer.

## Manage the study after sharing

Use the study actions menu to:

- copy the link again
- duplicate a study
- pause collection
- archive a study
- delete a study

Only delete a study when you are sure the data is no longer needed.
