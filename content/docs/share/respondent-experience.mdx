---
title: Respondent experience
description: Understand what participants need before they complete a Lemma study.
---

Participants should understand three things before they answer:

1. why they are being asked
2. whether the study is text or voice
3. that Lemma may ask follow-up questions

The experience should feel like a short guided conversation, not a long form.

## Set expectations in the invite

Good invite language:

> We are using Lemma to understand your experience in more depth than a static form can capture. You will answer a few questions, and Lemma may ask follow-ups based on what you say.

## Help participants give better answers

Ask for concrete experience, not opinions in the abstract.

Good prompt language:

- Describe what happened.
- Give an example if you can.
- Tell us what you expected instead.
- Share what would have made this easier.

Weak prompt language:

- Tell us any thoughts.
- Please provide feedback.
- What do you think?
