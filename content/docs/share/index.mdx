---

Launch work is about getting the right people into the study with the right expectation.

A Lemma study should feel like a lightweight link for participants, but it can collect evidence closer to a short interview. Set that expectation when you share it.

## Launch Checklist

<Steps>
<Step>

### Confirm the study goal

Before sending the link, check that the title, prompts, format, and follow-up depth match the decision you need to make.

</Step>
<Step>

### Choose the audience

Send the study directly, or use Contacts and Segments when you need a managed participant list.

</Step>
<Step>

### Explain the commitment

Tell participants what the study is about, why their answer matters, and whether they should expect a text or voice conversation.

</Step>
<Step>

### Monitor responses

Use the study Responses table to see completed sessions, summaries, transcripts, and answer columns as they come in.

</Step>
</Steps>
