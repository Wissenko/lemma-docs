Lemma bills and tracks usage per workspace.

The audited Account & Billing page distinguished:

- audio credits
- text interviews
- plan-level monthly or lifetime allowances

## Important distinction

**Audio credits** apply to voice conversations.

**Text interviews** apply to text conversation usage.

The exact credit consumption rules should be confirmed before publishing detailed pricing math.

## Credit notifications

Use notification settings to get alerts when:

- credits reach 50%
- credits reach 20%
- credits are depleted

## Current note

The product audit observed plan allowances, but did not verify credit consumption per completed study, per minute, or per session. Keep this page high-level until billing rules are confirmed.
