## When To Use Workspace Settings

Use Workspace settings when:

- the organization name is wrong
- you need to invite teammates
- you need to inspect who has access
- you need to understand member roles

## What The Audited Screen Included

The audited screen included:

- organization name
- Save button
- Team Members section
- invite members
- member search
- member role table

<HelpCallout type="note" title="Workspace settings are shared">
Changes here can affect how the workspace appears to teammates. Use Profile for personal account details.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Where do I change my personal name?

Use [Profile](/account/profile), not Workspace settings.

### Where do I invite teammates?

Use the Team Members area in Workspace settings, then review [How do I invite and manage teammates?](/account/team-members).

### What should I check before a team study?

Confirm the organization name, member access, and notification owner before launch.
