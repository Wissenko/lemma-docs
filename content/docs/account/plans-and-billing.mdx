Plans and billing are managed per workspace.

The audited screen showed:

- current workspace plan
- renewal date
- billing portal button
- monthly/annual toggle
- Free, Tools, Pro, and Scale plan cards
- upgrade buttons

## Manage payments and invoices

Use **Manage payments & invoices** to open the billing portal.

## Choose a plan

Choose a plan based on:

- audio credit needs
- expected text interview volume
- whether the workspace needs Chat, Doc, Slides, or other advanced outputs
- team usage

## Current note

Plan cards mentioned **Chat, Doc, Slides & Wiki**. The audit verified Chat, Doc, and Slides surfaces in the example study, but did not verify a Wiki surface. Do not document Wiki as live until confirmed.
