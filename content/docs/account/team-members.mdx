Use Team Members to manage who can access the workspace.

The audited screen showed:

- Invite Members
- member search
- a table with user, email, and role

## Before inviting someone

Confirm:

- the person should have access to study data
- the workspace is the right workspace
- their role should match what they need to do

## Current note

The audit saw many `participant` roles in the table, but did not confirm the full role model. Do not publish a complete role-permission matrix until product behavior is verified.
