## Main Navigation

The left sidebar includes:

- **New Study**: create a new study.
- **Studies**: search, open, duplicate, pause, archive, or delete studies.
- **Contacts**: manage participants, imports, segments, topics, and properties.
- **Workspace**: manage organization settings and team members.
- **Account & Billing**: manage profile, notifications, credits, and usage.
- **Support**: open support chat.

## Study-Level Navigation

Inside a published study, the audited example showed:

- **Responses**
- **Doc**
- **Slides**
- **View survey**

The Responses area also included:

- **Overview**
- **Sessions**
- **Chat**

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Where do I create a study?

Use **New Study** from the sidebar.

### Where do I find completed participant answers?

Open the study, then go to **Responses**. Use transcripts when you need the source wording behind a summary.

### Where do I manage teammates and organization settings?

Use **Workspace** for organization-level settings and team access. Use **Account & Billing** for profile, notifications, credits, and usage.
