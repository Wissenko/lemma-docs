Start here if you are new to Lemma or deciding whether a workflow belongs in Lemma.

The product is best understood as an adaptive study system. You create a study, people complete sessions, and Lemma helps turn those sessions into evidence your team can inspect and share.

## Good First Use Cases

- customer feedback that needs reasons and examples
- lead qualification that needs buying context before a call
- churn, cancellation, NPS, or CSAT follow-up where the first answer is too thin
- product research or message testing where quotes and objections matter
- client intake or stakeholder discovery where context saves a live call

## When Not To Use Lemma

Use a standard form tool when the job is only to collect a simple field, take payment, upload a file, run a quiz, or route people through complex form logic. Use Lemma when the answer matters enough to deserve follow-up.
