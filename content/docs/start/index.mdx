## What You Should Know

<HelpCallout type="note" title="Core terms">
- **Study**: the project you create in Lemma. It includes the goal, conversation type, follow-up depth, context, and questions.
- **Session**: one completed participant conversation.
- **Responses**: the table where you review sessions, summaries, transcripts, and answers.
- **Transcript**: the source of truth for what happened in a session.
- **Insights**: the report-style summary of the study.
- **Research**: the place to ask questions across the study data.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Should my first study be voice or text?

Use voice when nuance, stories, emotion, or hesitation matter. Use text when participants need a quieter written path.
