Lemma helps you run adaptive studies in text or voice.

Use it when the first answer is not enough. Lemma can ask follow-up questions so you can collect reasons, examples, tradeoffs, and quotes.

## How Lemma Works

<TwoColumn>
  <Panel title="Study">
    The thing you create in Lemma. It contains the goal, format, follow-up depth, context, and questions.
  </Panel>
  <Panel title="Session">
    One completed participant conversation inside a study.
  </Panel>
  <Panel title="Responses">
    The table where you review sessions, summaries, transcripts, and answers.
  </Panel>
  <Panel title="Insights and Research">
    The places where you synthesize results and ask questions across the study data.
  </Panel>
</TwoColumn>

## When To Use Lemma

Lemma is useful when you need more than a static answer.

Common workflows:

- customer discovery
- customer satisfaction
- product or service feedback
- employee experience

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Is Lemma only for voice?

No. Studies can use text or voice.

### Does Lemma write the study for me?

Lemma can draft the study from your context. You should still review the context, questions, and follow-up settings before using it.
