Lemma is an AI interview and study platform.

Use it when a static form would collect the first answer, but your team needs the reason, example, objection, tradeoff, or quote behind that answer.

## When To Use Lemma

Lemma is strongest when the useful answer appears after a follow-up.

Common workflows:

- customer discovery
- customer satisfaction
- product or service feedback
- churn and cancellation follow-up
- lead qualification
- employee experience
- message testing
- client or stakeholder discovery

<HelpCallout type="insight" title="The product is not just the question">
The core value is the adaptive follow-up. Lemma should help you capture the why, example, objection, tradeoff, and quote behind the first response.
</HelpCallout>

## How Lemma Differs From Static Forms

Static forms are good for structured fields and operational collection.

Lemma is better when you need:

- a conversation instead of a single text box
- follow-up questions
- transcripts and summaries
- themes and quotes
- Doc and Slides outputs
- a Research Agent that can answer questions about the study data

## What Lemma Is Not Trying To Replace First

Use a standard form builder when you primarily need:

- a simple signup form
- payment collection
- file upload
- quiz scoring
- exact branching-logic parity
- heavily branded embedded website forms

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Is Lemma only for research teams?

No. Research is one workflow, but Lemma can also support customer feedback, sales qualification, churn follow-up, internal feedback, and customer proof capture.

### Does every study need voice?

No. Voice is useful when spoken nuance matters. Text is useful when participation needs to be quieter or lower-friction.
