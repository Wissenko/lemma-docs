Topics are subscription categories for participants.

The app describes them as categories participants can opt in or out of. Distribution can use topics to gate sends to relevant audiences only.

## When To Use Topics

Use topics when you need subscription-style categories such as:

- UX research
- pricing
- beta programs
- customer stories
- product feedback

## How To Create A Topic

The topic form includes:

- key
- label
- description

<HelpCallout type="note" title="Topics are not the same as properties">
Use topics for participant subscription categories. Use properties for structured attributes that live on a contact record.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Should topics describe the study or the person?

Topics should describe the kind of communication or study category a participant can opt in or out of.

### What are good topic keys?

Use stable, readable keys like `ux-research`, `pricing`, `beta`, or `customer-stories`.

### What should I use for targeting attributes?

Use [properties](/contacts/properties) for attributes such as role, company size, plan tier, or region.
