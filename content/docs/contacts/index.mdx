Use Contacts when the quality of the audience matters as much as the study design.

Contacts is the participant database for your workspace. It helps you import people, add contacts manually, build segments, define subscription topics, and store custom properties for targeting.

<Outcome title="Audience principle">
A good study is not only well written. It reaches people who can answer from real experience.
</Outcome>

## Contacts vs participants

A **contact** is a person stored in your workspace database.

A **participant** is a person who completes a study session.

The same contact can be invited to multiple studies.

## Topics And Properties

Use **Topics** for subscription categories participants can opt in or out of. Use **Properties** for structured attributes you control, such as role, plan, company size, renewal date, or customer tier.
