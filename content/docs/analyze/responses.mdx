## How To Review Responses

<Steps>
<Step>

### Step 1: Open the study

Open the study from the Studies dashboard.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 2: Open Responses

Use the **Responses** tab to view completed sessions.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 3: Scan the table

Review participant, started time, duration, status, summary, transcript, and answer columns.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 4: Open a transcript when needed

Use the transcript link when you need exact wording or context.

</Step>
</Steps>

## What To Look For

- completed sessions
- participant summaries
- transcript links
- per-question answers
- patterns that appear across multiple sessions
- one-off details worth checking in the transcript

## FAQs & Common Issues

### What does the summary column do?

It gives a quick view of a session. Use the transcript when you need to verify the exact source wording.

### Why are there many answer columns?

Each question can appear as its own answer column, so the table may be wider than the screen.

### Where do I go after Responses?

Open Insights for synthesis or Research to ask a specific question across the study data.
