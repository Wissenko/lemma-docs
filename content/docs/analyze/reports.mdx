In Lemma, analysis happens in several surfaces:

- **Responses** for session-level review
- **Transcripts** for source evidence
- **Research Agent** for questions across study data
- **Doc** for a structured research document
- **Slides** for a presentation-style output

## Start with Responses

Use the Sessions table to see what came in and inspect individual sessions.

## Then Use Outputs

Open **Doc** when you need a research-style artifact with figures, quotes, and recommendations.

Open **Slides** when you need a presentation-style output.

Open **Chat** when you want to ask the Research Agent a question about the study.

## What good analysis should produce

A good study should help you say something more specific than "customers are unhappy."

Good output:

- "Three customers cancelled after setup friction created a two-week delay."
- "Pricing was not the first reason; it became a reason after onboarding failed."
- "The strongest quote is about losing trust after repeated manual workarounds."
