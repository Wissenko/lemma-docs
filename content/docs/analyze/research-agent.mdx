The Research Agent is a study-level chat surface.

It can answer questions about interview transcripts, reports, and analyses for the current study.

## What you can ask

The audited example suggested:

- What are the main themes of the responses?
- Are there any contradictions between participants?
- What unexpected findings came up?
- Write an executive summary memo.
- How do different participants compare in their responses?

## When to use it

Use the Research Agent when:

- there are many completed sessions
- you need a faster synthesis
- you want to compare participants
- you want contradictions or surprises
- you need a memo or summary draft

## Best prompts

Ask specific questions tied to a decision.

Good:

> Which participant quotes best explain why users trust AI for drafting but not pricing decisions?

Weak:

> What are the insights?
