---
title: Product limits
description: Avoid claiming unsupported capabilities in public docs.
---

Do not claim a feature is live unless it has been verified in the app.

## Verified in the product audit

The May 19, 2026 audit verified:

- studies dashboard
- new study screen
- text and voice conversation formats
- follow-up depth options
- brain dump field
- discussion guide upload button
- study templates
- contacts
- contact imports
- topics
- properties
- segments
- response table
- transcript route
- Doc output
- Slides output
- Research Agent chat surface
- account notifications
- credits and plans
- workspace/team settings
- support widget

## Not verified yet

Do not document these as live until confirmed:

- working participant preview for the read-only example
- exact export file formats
- export credit behavior
- Research Agent credit behavior
- shareable report links
- full workspace role matrix
- Wiki surface
- exact billing math per voice minute or session

## Avoid unsupported form-builder parity

Do not claim parity with Typeform or Google Forms for:

- importing Typeform or Google Forms
- branching logic parity
- file uploads
- payments
- quizzes or scoring
- advanced themes and design controls
- embedded website forms
- hidden fields
- native CRM writes
- automated workflow actions

Use Lemma for high-value qualitative studies where follow-up questions and outputs matter.
