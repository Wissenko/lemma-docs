## Available Templates

The observed templates are:

- **Customer Discovery**: use this to learn what people need before they choose you.
- **Customer Satisfaction**: use this to learn why customers are happy, frustrated, or leaving.
- **Product/Service Feedback**: use this to learn what to improve, add, or remove.
- **Employee Experience**: use this to learn what is making employees stay, struggle, or leave.

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Should I use a template or Smart Builder?

Use a template when the workflow matches your goal. Use Smart Builder when your context is more specific or less structured.

### Do templates create a final study?

No. Treat a template as a starting point. Review the draft before using it.

### Can I still change follow-up depth after choosing a template?

Review the study settings and question-level follow-ups before using the study.
