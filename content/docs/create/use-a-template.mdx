Templates help you avoid a blank start.

The New Study screen includes preset templates for common workflows.

Observed templates:

- Customer Discovery
- Customer Satisfaction
- Product/Service Feedback
- Employee Experience

## Choose the closest job

Use **Customer Discovery** to learn what people need before they choose you.

Use **Customer Satisfaction** to understand why customers are happy, frustrated, or leaving.

Use **Product/Service Feedback** to collect deeper feedback on a product, service, store experience, onboarding flow, package, feature, or offer.

Use **Employee Experience** to understand onboarding friction, engagement drivers, manager pain points, process blockers, and exit reasons.

## Customize the template

After choosing a template, make it specific:

- name the audience
- add the workflow or product context
- remove questions that do not support the decision
- add follow-up targets that matter for your use case
- choose the right interaction format and depth
