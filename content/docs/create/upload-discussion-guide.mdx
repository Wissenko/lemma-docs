If you already have a discussion guide, upload it instead of recreating it by hand.

The New Study screen includes **Upload Discussion Guide** for PDF guides.

## When to use this

Use a discussion guide when:

- the research plan already exists
- a customer, client, or teammate approved the guide
- the order of questions matters
- the study needs to preserve specific language

Use a brain dump or template when:

- you are still shaping the study
- you want Lemma to help generate the first draft
- you only know the goal and audience

## What to check after import

After importing a guide, review:

- the study goal
- the opening questions
- the follow-up depth
- the participant-facing language
- whether the guide assumes a human moderator

Human-moderated guides often contain notes that should become guidance for Lemma, not participant-facing questions.
