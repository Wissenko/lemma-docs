## How To Use Smart Builder

<Steps>
<Step>

### Step 1: Open New Study

Click **New Study** from the sidebar.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 2: Choose the interaction format

Choose **Text conversation** or **Voice conversation**.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 3: Choose follow-up depth

Choose **Quick pulse**, **Standard interview**, or **Deep dive**.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 4: Add your brain dump

Describe the audience, what you want to learn, and any constraints. You can write this roughly.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 5: Review the draft

Review the generated study goal, background, tone, key terms, welcome message, closing message, and questions.

</Step>
</Steps>

## What To Include

- who should answer
- what you want to learn
- what context Lemma should know
- any terms, product names, team names, or acronyms
- what kind of follow-up would be useful

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Does the brain dump need to be polished?

No. It can be rough. Smart Builder is designed to turn messy context into a usable draft.

### What happens after Smart Builder drafts the study?

Review the generated context and questions. Then edit anything that feels too generic or too broad.

### Should I add key terms?

Add proper nouns, product names, team names, or acronyms that Lemma should recognize.
