## Options

<TwoColumn>
  <Panel title="Quick pulse">
    A short pulse check: 3-5 questions with minimal follow-ups. Use it when you need a fast read.
  </Panel>
  <Panel title="Standard interview">
    A typical research interview: 6-10 questions with light probing. Use it for most first studies.
  </Panel>
  <Panel title="Deep dive">
    An in-depth exploration: 12-18 questions with rich probing. Use it when nuance matters more than speed.
  </Panel>
</TwoColumn>

## How to choose

Use **Quick pulse** for:

- quick satisfaction checks
- lightweight feedback
- early signal gathering

Use **Standard interview** for:

- customer discovery
- product/service feedback
- lead qualification
- onboarding or churn follow-up

Use **Deep dive** for:

- strategic research
- customer story discovery
- employee experience investigations
- high-stakes qualitative decisions

## Common mistake

Do not choose Deep dive just because the topic is important. Choose it when the participant has enough context and motivation to answer a longer conversation.
