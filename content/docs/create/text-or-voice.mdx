## How To Choose The Format

<Steps>
<Step>

### Step 1: Open New Study

Start from the New Study screen.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 2: Open the interaction menu

Use the **Interaction** control.

</Step>
<Step>

### Step 3: Choose Text conversation or Voice conversation

Choose the format that fits the participant and the kind of answer you need.

</Step>
</Steps>

## When To Use Voice

Use **Voice conversation** when spoken detail matters. Voice is useful for stories, examples, hesitation, emotion, or longer answers.

## When To Use Text

Use **Text conversation** when participants need a quieter written path or when voice is not the right fit for the audience.

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Does voice change the study setup?

You still review the same core pieces: context, questions, follow-up depth, and instructions.

### Can text studies still be adaptive?

Yes. Text studies can still use follow-up questions.

### Should every study use voice?

No. Choose the format that makes it easiest for participants to give useful answers.
