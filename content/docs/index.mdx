## FAQs & Common Issues

### Is Lemma a form builder?

Lemma can replace qualitative forms, but it is not trying to match every static form-builder feature. Use it when the answer needs follow-up.

### Where should a new user start?

Start with [How do I create my first study?](/start/quickstart), then read [How do I design a study that gets useful answers?](/create) before launching anything important.

### What should I open after participants respond?

Open [How do I review responses and understand the evidence?](/analyze).
