Use these docs when you want to turn a question into evidence your team can use.

Lemma is organized around a simple self-serve workflow: design the study, reach the right people, collect sessions, understand the evidence, and share findings. The app calls each adaptive form or interview a **study**. People complete **sessions**. Completed sessions become Responses, transcripts, Research Agent answers, Doc, Slides, and exports.

<Outcome title="The main job">
Use Lemma when a static form would collect an answer, but the real value is in the follow-up: the reason, example, objection, tradeoff, quote, or next step behind that answer.
</Outcome>

## The Core Loop

<Steps>
<Step>

### Define the decision

Start with the decision the study should support. A strong study asks for evidence your team can act on, not generic feedback.

</Step>
<Step>

### Design the adaptive study

Choose text or voice, choose follow-up depth, and give Lemma enough context to ask useful follow-up questions.

</Step>
<Step>

### Collect sessions

Share the study link or target participants from Contacts and Segments.

</Step>
<Step>

### Synthesize the evidence

Use Responses, transcripts, Chat, Doc, and Slides to move from individual answers to decision-ready findings.

</Step>
</Steps>
