## Contacts vs Participants

A **contact** is a person stored in your workspace database.

A **participant** is a person who completes a study session.

The same contact can be invited to multiple studies.

## Topics And Properties

Use **Topics** for subscription categories participants can opt in or out of. Use **Properties** for structured attributes you control, such as role, plan, company size, renewal date, or customer tier.

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Do I need Contacts for every study?

No. You can share a study link manually. Use Contacts when you need reusable audiences, segmentation, or cleaner participant management.

### What should I define first: segments, topics, or properties?

Start with properties when you need structured targeting data. Use segments to combine rules into reusable audiences. Use topics for subscription categories.
