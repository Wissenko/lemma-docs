## Important Distinction

The audited Account & Billing page distinguished:

- audio credits
- text interviews
- plan-level monthly or lifetime allowances

**Audio credits** apply to voice conversations.

**Text interviews** apply to text conversation usage.

The exact credit consumption rules should be confirmed before publishing detailed pricing math.

## Credit Notifications

Use notification settings to get alerts when:

- credits reach 50%
- credits reach 20%
- credits are depleted

<HelpCallout type="note" title="Keep this page high-level for now">
The product audit observed plan allowances, but did not verify credit consumption per completed study, per minute, or per session.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Are audio credits and text interviews the same thing?

No. Audio credits apply to voice conversations. Text interviews apply to text conversation usage.

### Where are credits managed?

Credits and usage are managed at the workspace level.

### What should I set before launching a large study?

Set [notifications](/account/notifications) so the workspace gets credit and response alerts.
