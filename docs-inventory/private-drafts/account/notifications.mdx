## What You Can Monitor

The audited Account & Billing page included notification settings for:

- credits at 50%
- credits at 20%
- credits depleted
- study paused
- new responses

## Response Frequency

The observed new-response frequency options were:

- Every response
- Daily summary
- Weekly summary
- Never

## Recommended Settings

Use **Every response** for small active studies where each session matters.

Use **Daily summary** when the study has moderate volume.

Use **Weekly summary** when you only need a periodic digest.

Use **Never** only when another teammate owns monitoring.

<HelpCallout type="tip" title="Assign notification ownership">
Before launching a study, decide who is responsible for noticing new responses and credit warnings.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Which setting should I use for a live customer study?

Use Every response or Daily summary, depending on volume and urgency.

### Should everyone receive every response?

No. Send high-frequency alerts to the study owner, then use summaries for teammates who only need periodic updates.
