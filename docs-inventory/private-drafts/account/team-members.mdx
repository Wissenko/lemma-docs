## Before Inviting Someone

Confirm:

- the person should have access to study data
- the workspace is the right workspace
- their role should match what they need to do

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Who should be invited?

Invite teammates who need to design studies, monitor responses, review evidence, or manage workspace operations.

### Should participants be workspace members?

Usually no. Participants should use the respondent-facing study link unless they also need workspace access.
