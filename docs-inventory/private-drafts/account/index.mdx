## Before You Launch Larger Studies

Confirm:

- the workspace name is correct
- the right teammates have access
- response notifications are set
- credit and usage alerts are enabled
- the study owner knows where to monitor responses

<HelpCallout type="note" title="Workspace settings affect shared work">
Profile settings are personal. Workspace settings, team members, credits, and usage affect the broader team.
</HelpCallout>

## FAQs & Common Issues

### Do workspace settings change my personal profile?

No. Workspace settings describe the organization. Profile settings describe your personal account.

### Who should receive notifications?

At least one person who owns the active study should receive response and credit notifications.
